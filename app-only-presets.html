Presets (App Only)

The website keeps things fast and simple. To customize presets for your department, use the FireOps Calc app.

In the app, you can create and save presets locally on the device for:

Hose Lines

  • Line 1 / Line 2 / Line 3 (labels, diameter, friction factors)
  • Nozzle type (fog / smooth), target GPM, nozzle pressure
  • Common working lengths for quick picks

Standpipes

  • Floor/height/elevation rules
  • Outlet pressure targets & safety margins
  • Department friction values & packs

Sprinklers

  • System types (wet, dry, preaction)
  • Target pressures and starting flows
  • Risers/FD connections and notes

Foam

  • Concentrate % and eductor settings
  • Required flows for hazards
  • Pickup points and inventory

Monitors

  • Nozzle tips & flow tables
  • Deployment presets (roof, ground, portable)
  • Pressure caps & safety notes

Blitz Lines

  • Tip size / GPM presets (e.g., 1⅜, 1½)
  • PDP targets for short/long lays
  • Preferred hose combos (2½, 3")
