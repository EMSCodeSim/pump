FireOps Calc FAQ

Quick answers for firefighters, engineers, instructors, and driver/operator students.

What is FireOps Calc?

FireOps Calc is a firefighter-built fire pump calculator and training website for pump pressure, friction loss, water supply, relay pumping, printable pump cards, and driver/operator practice.

Who is FireOps Calc for?

It is designed for firefighters, engineers, apparatus operators, fire instructors, fire academies, and students learning pump operations.

Does FireOps Calc replace department SOPs?

No. FireOps Calc is a calculator, reference, and training aid. Always follow your department SOPs, instructor direction, manufacturer data, and local requirements.

Are the Apple, Android, and web versions the same?

Not exactly. The website is being built into the public calculator and training hub. The iPhone and Android apps may contain different feature sets while development continues.
