FAQ

Common FireOps Calc questions.

Short answers help users understand the tools and give search engines clear page context.

What is FireOps Calc?

A firefighter-built pump calculation and driver/operator training hub with calculators, print tools, scenarios, flashcards, and how-to guides.

Is this an official certification question bank?

No. The questions and scenarios are original practice material for study and instruction. Use your department SOPs and instructor direction.

Can I print training material?

Yes. Use the pump card creator, printable scenarios, friction loss table, flashcard print views, and instructor packs with answer keys.

Do the web and app versions match exactly?

The website is the public tool hub and testing ground. Apple and Android app features may differ while new tools are being added.
