FireOps Calc FAQ

What is FireOps Calc?

FireOps Calc is a firefighter-built fire pump calculator and training tool for pump pressure, friction loss, water supply, relay pumping, and driver/operator practice.

Who is FireOps Calc for?

It is designed for firefighters, engineers, apparatus operators, officers, fire instructors, fire academies, and students learning pump operations.

Does FireOps Calc replace department SOPs?

No. FireOps Calc is a calculator, reference, and training aid. Always follow your department SOPs, instructor direction, manufacturer data, and local requirements.

Can I use the calculator on the web?

Yes. Open the web calculator from this page. The iPhone and Android versions are also available for mobile use.
