Custom Settings (App Only)

Department-specific presets (Line 1/2/3 and nozzle presets) are available in the FireOps Calc app. The website version keeps calculations fast and simple, but does not store custom presets.

Install the app to define your hoses, nozzles, target GPM/NP, friction factors, and common lengths. Your choices are saved locally on your device for quick, offline use.
