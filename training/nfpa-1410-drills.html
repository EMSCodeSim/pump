How to use these drills

Pick one skill.Keep the drill focused: first line, water supply, FDC, master stream, or relay.
Set local benchmarks.Define time targets, staffing, hose loads, PPE, and safety requirements using your SOP.
Run the evolution.Record water established, line charged, target pressure, communications, and safety issues.
Debrief immediately.Ask what went well, what slowed the crew, and what the engineer would change.

Evaluator focus

  • Correct apparatus placement and pump engagement.
  • Water supply established or planned before sustained flow.
  • Target PDP selected from calculator, pump card, or SOP.
  • Hose deployment is safe, organized, and free of major kinks.
  • Clear communication between crew and pump operator.
  • Safety issues corrected before increasing flow.
