FireOps Calc – Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 15, 2025
Developer: EMSCodeSim

1. Introduction

FireOps Calc is a utility application designed for fire service pump operations, hose calculations, and training use. This app does not collect, store, share, sell, or transmit any personal or sensitive user data.

2. Information We Collect

FireOps Calc does not collect any of the following information:

  • Personal information (such as name, email address, phone number, or postal address)
  • Photos, contacts, files, or any content from your device
  • Device identifiers, advertising IDs, or analytics data
  • Location data (GPS or network-based location)

The app performs all calculations locally on your device and does not send data to any server controlled by EMSCodeSim or third parties.

3. Data Sharing

Because FireOps Calc does not collect data, no data is shared with any third party. We do not integrate third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking libraries.

4. Children’s Privacy

FireOps Calc is intended for professional firefighters, engineers, and emergency services personnel. It is not directed toward children and is not designed for use by individuals under the age of 13. Since the app does not collect data, it does not knowingly collect information from children.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time to reflect changes in the app or applicable laws. Any updates will be posted on this page with an updated “Last updated” date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
