Foam eductor setup

For many inline eductors, pump pressure is based on the required eductor inlet pressure plus loss before the eductor. Do not add nozzle pressure after the eductor to the pump pressure unless your manufacturer/SOP directs it.

Setup warnings

  • Match nozzle GPM to the eductor rating.
  • Keep hose after the eductor within manufacturer limits.
  • Make sure the pickup tube is seated and the nozzle is fully open.
  • Flush the eductor and hose after use.
